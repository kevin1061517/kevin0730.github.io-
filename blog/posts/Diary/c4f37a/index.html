My 2022 Look Back

發表於 2023-01-21 分類於 Diary Disqus：

As 2022 came to a close, I summarized the 5 favorite things last year.
Hope I can learned a lesson from these experience.

Get Started with mountain climbing

No matter how hard the road lies ahead, as long as you're in the right direction

When I was a child, I often went hiking with my family nearly twice a month. But all of these trails are easy, it might take me around 30-40 minutes total to go up and down. Therefore, I regarded easy hiking as the exercise for the elderly, and it didn't seem to arouse my any interest.

I bought the mountaineering boots that cost me around 5000 dollars last January since I got a chance to go to Taipingshan in Yilan with my friend, and we planed to hike in the world's most beautiful trails named Jianqing Huaigu Trail. Afterwards, my friends asked me to go climbing Taman which is the highest hill of New Taipei City's all peaks. I guessed I gradually love mountain climbing at that time.

Right now, I've reached the summit of five of top 100 Mountains in Taiwan including the three-peak mountain in Hehuan, the Tao Mountain and the Kalaye Mountain. To be honest, I feel that mountain make me small and inspire me to stop worring about things you can control. My favorite things during mountain climbing is not only having a great time with friends but also stopted at the top of the hill to admire the breathtaking scenery.

Speak English with confidence

When you speak a different language, you open a window into a new culture

Last September, I started taking classes twice a week on Engoo which is an online English tutor platform. With conversations with various tutors from the Filipinas, Panama, Serbia, etc, I felt that the English is the best tool to understand culture and meet interesting people from different countries. The tutor I usually choose comes from Filipinas. she just a college student and English is her second language, but her English speaking sound more like a native speaker. What mostly impressed me was that she never been aboard and her way to improve English speaking is to watch American TV show over again and again and repeat loudly the exact words what she just hear on drama. Therefore, I learned lots of tips for speaking English from her and nowadays I believe I get more confidence in English speaking.

Start to workout and stick to it

The first step is always the hardest

First half of 2022, I started to work out at the gym one or twice a week. But every time I just trained biceps or triceps and that is. You can imagine that this way didn't build any muscles for me. Last November, I found a workout coach to guide me through the correct positioning and form. After tens of workout coach classes in December, I'm getting used to working out by myself or with my friends. I think that I improve my strength and flexibility a lot and keep gaining more muscle as soon as possible.

Prepare for a Google interview

If at first you do not succeed, try, try again

Last March, I accidently got a chance for a Google interview. Before the interview, HR arranged an appointment for me to talk with Google engineer. After conversation, I totally understanded why Google's company culture and benefit are always the major reasons people would like to work at Google. And then I tried my best to prepare for the interview questions and coding interviews through Leetcode. Unfortunately, I finally got rejected due to the accuracy and quality of the solution. Frankly, this interview experience let me know I should spend more time in practicing Leetcode problems and Data Structures & Algorithms.

Have a great time with Her

Not every encounter has an end. But each meeting carries some meanings

The almost whole of last year, I've had a crush on a girl and I tried pretty hard to pursue her. As the saying goes, the more desperately we want something, the less likely we are of getting it. Although I went on dates with her several times, in my heart of hearts I felt that she didn't want to be in a relationship with me. However, I though twice about this situation, I gave too much attention to her and couldn't help stopping thinking about her then. Obviously I should spend too much time on myself and improve self-worth.

Maybe I will keep looking for a person who would encourage me, inspire me, support me, keep me focus, make me happy and give me unconditional love.
