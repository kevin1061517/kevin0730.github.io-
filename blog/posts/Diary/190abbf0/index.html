Time To Get Over It

發表於 2023-04-01 分類於 Diary Disqus：

A lot has happened these day.
I planned to resign from my poisition, ended a chemistry in a relationship, rejected a girl who likes me, met a new friend who works abroad as a software engineer and got another job interview opportunity in Australia.
What an incredible month.

Something that has been bothering me a lot is that my emotions have been heavily affected by the chemistry in a relationship for over a year. I find it weird and mysterious why my mood is like a roller coaster, depending on her reactions and responses to me.
Therefore, I decided to end this relationship even though she wanted to be a friend with me. However, she never know how hard it is for me to not text her, and how difficult it is to keep myself busy enough to stop thinking of her. Even now, I dream of her sometimes and I fucking miss her :(
Maybe, there is a type of love called no contact.

I wrote this article to memorialize my feelings right now, and I believe I will get over them to go after what I want.
